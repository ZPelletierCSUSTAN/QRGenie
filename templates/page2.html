Instructions

  • Step 1: Go to the QR Code Generator or Batch QR Code Generator tab.
  • Step 2: Enter text. On the Batch page, you can enter multiple lines (up to 10). Press CRTL + ENTER to make a new line.
  • Step 3: Choose your QR Color and Shape it will shows up in the live preview. In the Batch page choose Apply Setting To All QR codes or edit them individually.
  • Step 4: Click Generate. On the Batch page, you will see a grid of previews.
  • Step 5: Click the Red 'X' to remove codes you don't want. Click "Edit" on a card to change just that specific code. Click "Save" to download.
