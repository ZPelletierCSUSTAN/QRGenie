Instructions

  • Step 1: Go to the QR Code Generator or Batch QR Code Generator tab.
  • Step 2: Enter text. On the Batch page, you can enter multiple lines (up to 10).
  • Step 3: When you choose your QR code Color and Shape it will show up in the live preview. In the Batch page choose Apply Setting To All QR codes or edit them individually.
  • Step 4: Click Generate. On the Batch page, you will see a grid of previews. If there is issues with your live preview then refresh the page.
  • Step 5: Click the Red 'X' to remove codes you don't want. Click "Edit" on a card to change just that specific code. Click "Save" to download.
