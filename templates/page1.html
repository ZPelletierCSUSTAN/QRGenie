Instructions

  • Step 1: Go to the QR Code Generator or Batch QR Code Generator tab.
  • Step 2: Enter a URL (e.g., https://google.com) or plain text.
  • Step 3: Use the sliders to increase the image size (resolution) and border width.
  • Step 4: Pick a color for QR code and its border (Dark colors on white backgrounds scan best). You can also select different shapes (Rounded, Circles, etc.).
  • Step 5: Click "Generate" (if in Batch mode) and then "Save" to download your generated QR code as a PNG.
